The Rules

The game is simple. You will be asked wether to type in the colour of a word or the word itself.
For example, the correct answer to 'Write the colour Blue' would be Orange, where as 'Write the word Blue' would be Blue.
You have 20 seconds to score as many as possible. Changing difficulty effects how long you have to answer each question before it changes but awards more points for higher difficulties. Good luck!
