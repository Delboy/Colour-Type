The Rules

The game is simple. You will be asked whether to type in the colour of a word or the word itself.
For example, the correct answer to
'Type the colour Blue'
would be Orange, where as;
'Type the word Blue'
would be Blue.
You have 20 seconds to score as many as possible. Changing difficulty affects how long you have to answer each question before it changes. Higher difficulties will award more points per question. Good luck!

How to play 

Difficulty :

Easy awards 1 point per correct answer.
Normal awards 2 points per correct answer.
Hard awards 3 points per correct answer.
